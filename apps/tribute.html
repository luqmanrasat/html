Monkey D. Luffy

The Man Who Will Become The Pirate King

Luffy after timeskip
  • Monkey D. Luffy, also known as "Straw Hat Luffy" and commonly as "Straw Hat"
  • The main protagonist of the manga and anime, One Piece.
  • The founder and captain of the Straw Hat Pirates.
  • His lifelong dream is to become the Pirate King
  • The son of the Revolutionary leader Monkey D. Dragon.
  • The paternal grandson of the Marine hero Monkey D. Garp.
  • The sworn brother of late "Fire Fist" Portgas D. Ace and Revolutionary chief of staff Sabo.
  • The foster son of Curly Dadan.
